Manual Upload Preview:

  • Google Drive URL Upload option is pretty similar to Manual Upload option. In this process as well, Seller needs to download their transaction data reports from Amazon Seller Central as explained in Manual Upload process. To access our detailed step-by-step guidebook on fetching reports from Seller Central, please click on Generating Orders Report, Generating Returns Report or Generating Payments Report, as per requirement.

  • Only difference is instead of manually uploading transaction report files on to our website, Seller can save those reports on their personal/official Google Drive, and provide us with a publicly shareable URL/link to each file. Each shared file link should have a View permission attached (by default, shared files are Viewable unless modified) to it. Our system shall read data from those files and generate reports based on them.

  • Uploading files to Google Drive & sharing it's URL is extremely simple, but we understand that it might get little confusing for the first time. Hence we provide a detailed step-by-step guidebook to smoothen this process. Please click on How to upload a file on Google Drive and get shareable Link for reference.
