Manual Upload Preview:

  • Manual Upload option requires a Seller to download 3 specific reports from their Amazon Seller Central portal on to their personal/work computer. Once downloaded, Seller needs to upload these reports in respective category options below.

  • Required Amazon reports consist of Orders, Returns and Payments detail. Report generation in Amazon Seller Central demands a Seller to select a time-frame, so please ensure to generate all reports for same time-frame. Currently 90 days is the upper limit set by Amazon for Orders & 60 days for Returns data.

  • Ideally Amazon generates these files within 45 minutes, after receiving a request from Seller. Once generated by Amazon, these reports shall be visible in Seller portal with a Download option next to it.

  • Occasionally in Payments section within AMZ Seller Central, you might notice more than one Payment source. In such a scenario, you need to select and download payment transactions for desired time-frame from all the sources. Generally, utmost there are 2 Payment sources found [Example: Amazon Payments Inc and Amazon Services LLCBeta; or another example could be Electronic Transactions (Credit Card/Net Banking/GC) and COD Transactions and Non-Transactional Fees].

  • Generating & downloading above mentioned reports are extremely simple, but we understand that it might get little confusing for the first time. Hence we provide detailed step-by-step guidebook to smoothen this process. Please click on Generating Orders Report, Generating Returns Report or Generating Payments Report to reference required guidebook.

  • Data uploaded by Seller is only retained for next 24 hours by our system from the time of upload. After that time-frame, our highly alert system automatically destroys uploaded data from our database, and our system generated Dashboards and reports either won't be visible anymore or would contain stale/inaccurate metrics.

  • To again review Dashboards and other provided options, a Seller just needs to once again upload data for our system to generate reports. Please note that again data shall be deleted after above mentioned time-frame. This process has been implemented keeping privacy of seller business data in mind.

  • Our team is rigorously working on facilitating alternatives as well, like 'Google Drive URL Upload' and 'Amazon MWS Login' which shall be enabled soon. Once enabled, each Seller shall be timely notified regarding this addition.
